## Chuan Meng (孟 川)

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Information Retrieval Lab (IRLab) at the University of Amsterdam (UvA), promoter: Prof. dr. [Maarten de Rijke](https://staff.fnwi.uva.nl/m.derijke/bio/) and dr. [Mohammad Aliannejadi](https://aliannejadi.com/). 
I currently work on Information Retrieval and Natural Language Processing, especially focusing on **Conversational Information Seeking (CIS)** and **Query Performance Prediction (QPP)**.  

Before joining UvA, I received my master's degree from Shandong University in 2021, promoters: Prof. dr. [Zhumin Chen](http://ir.sdu.edu.cn/~zhuminchen/~zhuminchen_en.htm) and Prof. dr. [Pengjie Ren](https://pengjieren.github.io/). During my master's degree, I worked on **Knowledge-Grounded Conversation (KGC)**.
The **KGC paper list** that I maintained is [here](https://github.com/ChuanMeng/Knowledge-Grounded-Conversation).
